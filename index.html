University of Bonn

Biography

I am a Jordanian graduate from the master’s program “Plant Sciences” at the University of Bonn. I completed my thesis in the field of plant molecular stress physiology in which I characterized the gene expression profile of several ELIP genes in the desiccation tolerant Lindernia brevidens and its desiccation sensitive counterpart Lindernia subracemosa.

During my studies, I have developed profound knowledge in quantitative, transcriptomic, proteomic, microscopic, and genetic engineering techniques. Currently, I am keen to get more exposed to innovative research projects through employment or PhD studies.
