Biography

I am a Jordanian soon-to-be graduate from the master’s program “Computational Mechanics” at the Technical University of Munich. Right now, I am working on my thesis which is implementation of fourth order finite volume solver in the hydromechanics chair’s CFD in-house solver.

During my studies, I have developed a profound knowledge in Finite Element Analysis, Material Modeling and Computational Fluid Dynamics. Also, through working part-time in the industry, I have expanded my knowledge in agile software development. Currently, I am keen to get more exposed to innovative projects through full-time employment.
